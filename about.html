Beau Walters is a independent photographer based in Boulder, Colorado. Focused exclusively on black and white portraiture, he creates images that evoke feelings of nostalgia. It all started when he built a small darkroom in his basement apartment where he learned to develop film and create prints. This led to volunteering and eventually working at the University of Colorado in the analog photography and filmmaking departments. He is passionate about traditional photographic practices and the ways in which images can change the world.
